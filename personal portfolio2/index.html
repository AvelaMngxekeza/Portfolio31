About Me

My name is Avela Songezo Mngxekeza.

I'm a designer and developer looking to advance my career in the design field.

I was born and reared in Cape Town's Khayelitsha neighborhood. Growing up, I had a unique perspective on the world since I had relatives that supported my job choices. I realized that everyone who has a problem can solve it using technology combined with design for that thing!

I discovered a passion for design in eighth school and decided to major in MGD (Media Graphic Design) in college. I grew fascinated with the way people think, feel, and behave - and how products could help them live more satisfying lives - after skipping out due to my father's financial troubles at home. I create websites and mobile apps for individual people, clients, and the general public.

I am currently working as a UI/UX Designer (Intern) at Younglings Africa. I create websites and mobile apps for clients. I use Figma and have also learnt Adobe Illustrator. Before joining Younglings Africa, I spent six months at CodeTelligence NPO learning how to design websites and mobile apps as well as coding with html, CSS, and java script. Outside of design, I'm a huge battlerap fan, and I enjoy singing and writing poetry, as well as listening to music from the past to the present.

I have the time, I am always willing to participate. Contact me and let's talk!
